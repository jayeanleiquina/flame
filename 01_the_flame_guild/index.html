🔥 Campaign: The Right to Rest (and Respawn)

We are tired of being tired. Mages, Artisans, Laborers, and Engineers all deserve the right to rest and respawn without fear of exploitation.

Our 3 Main Campaign Goals

  1. The 4 hours rule - All members should have at least 4 hours of uninterrupted rest time between shifts.
  2. Universal Bedroll Access - Free, flameproof bedrolls for all guild-certified members.
  3. Respawn Leave - If you're resurrected due to unsafe working conditions, you get the day off. No arguments.

  • "No Stew, No Spells!
  • "Stop the Grind – Nap Time Now!"
